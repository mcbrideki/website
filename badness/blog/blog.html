Binary Star System

Bounce off each other's gravity
dance, radiant.
Sing a soundless song
(harmonic motion).
Together create an orbit
(group action).
But, with synchronicity comes apogee
love elipses . . .
Two stars still shine:
pressure glows.
My light looks brighter in your shadow.
